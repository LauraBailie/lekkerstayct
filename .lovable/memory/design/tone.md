---
name: App tone guidelines
description: Warm, professional Cape Town tone — limited slang, no "kak", sparse "bru"
type: preference
---
- Keep warm, proud Cape Town local feel but stay professional and approachable.
- No "kak" ever. Reduce "bru" — use "my friend" or "my bru" sparingly.
- Allowed slang (use naturally, not forced): Howzit, Lekker, Awê, Yoh, Eish, Sharp-sharp.
- Success toasts: "Lekker! Added to the community."
- Submission confirmations: "Yoh, submission recorded sharp-sharp!"
- Fair Price responses use "Yoh" or "Eish" occasionally.
- Overall tone: helpful, trustworthy, community-focused.
